Lasagna Recipe

Homepage

Ingredients

  1. 10 ounces lasagna noodles
  2. 1 ½ pounds lean ground beef
  3. 3 cups cottage cheese
  4. 2 eggs
  5. 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  6. 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  7. salt and pepper to taste
  8. 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  9. 2 (6.5 ounce) cans tomato sauce

How To Make

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
Step 2: Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil, add the lasagna noodles and let cook for 8 to 10 minutes || Drain
Step 3: Borwn the ground beef using a skillet
Step 4: Grab a bowl and mix the cottage cheese, garlic powder, parsley, eggs, salt and pepper using a fork
Step 5: In a seperate bowl combine the tomato paste and tomato sauce
Step 6: Grab a 9x13 inch baking pan layer the noodles, ground beef, tomato sauce mixture and cottag cheese mixture in that order. Only two layers will fit.
Final Step: Cover with tin foil and bake for 30 minutes, then remove the foil for the last 8 minutes of baking.
