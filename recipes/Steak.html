Steak Recipe

Homepage

Ingredients

  1. 1 Pound fresh steak (rib-eye, round or sirloin)*
  2. 1 Large yellow onion, sliced thin
  3. 3 Tablespoons Kikkoman Teriyaki Marinade & Sauce
  4. 4 soft sandwich rolls
  5. 1 (8 fl oz) jar cheese spread

How To Make

Step 1:  Add oil to a large non stick skillet
Step 2:  Saute onions and 2 tablespoons of teriyaki sauce until onions are soft
Step 3:  Add the meat and cook until the meat is browns slightly
Step 4:  Add the remaining tablespoon of teriyaki and stir to combine
Step 5:  Place the cheese on top and stir until melted, mixing everything together
Final Step:  Scoop the meat and place onto the buns
