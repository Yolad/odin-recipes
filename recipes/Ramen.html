Ramen Recipe

Homepage

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups water
  2. 1 (3 ounce) package any flavor ramen noodles
  3. 1 slice American cheese

How To Make

Step 1:Bring water to a boil in a saucepan.
Step 2: Add ramen noodles and cook until tender, about 2 minutes.
Final Step:  Pour out water, then stir in seasoning packet and cheese until well blended.
